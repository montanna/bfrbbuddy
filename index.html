There and Back Again: Reflecting on my Semester Abroad

Last January, my roommate and I packed our bags and set off on the adventure of a lifetime: our semester abroad in Preston, England. Preston is kind of like the Fresno of England, in that when exchange students show up there, the first question locals ask is usually "Why did you come here?"

It's a genuinely charming city with a lively downtown, a beautiful museum, and tons of historic buildings. But when we researched it beforehand, the biggest selling point we could find was that Preston has the cheapest pints anywhere in England.

We figured we'd feel right at home.

Reina (my roommate) and I decided to live with a local family rather than on campus at our new school, the University of Central Lancashire (also known as UCLan or "the other UCLA.")

When we arrived, our host, Mary, had the loveliest breakfast spread out for us in the morning.
